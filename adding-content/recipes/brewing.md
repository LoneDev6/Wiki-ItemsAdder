# Brewing

Brewing stand recipes are available through [ItemsAdderAdditions](https://itemsadderadditions.com/en/docs/recipes/brewing).

It adds a `brewing` recipe type that registers a Paper potion mix and supports exact matching of ItemsAdder, MMOItems, and vanilla items.

Check the full documentation [here](https://itemsadderadditions.com/en/docs/recipes/brewing).
