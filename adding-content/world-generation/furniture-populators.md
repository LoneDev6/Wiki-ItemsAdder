# Furniture Populators (ItemsAdderAdditions)

Furniture world generation is available through [ItemsAdderAdditions](https://itemsadderadditions.com/en/docs/worldgen/world-generation).

ItemsAdderAdditions provides two modes for spawning custom **furniture** in newly generated chunks:

* **World Populator** (`blocks_populators`): spawns furniture in vein-style clusters, similar to ore generation.
* **Surface Decorator** (`surface_decorators`): scatters furniture across the surface of newly generated terrain.

Check the full documentation [here](https://itemsadderadditions.com/en/docs/worldgen/world-generation).
